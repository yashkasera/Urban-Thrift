How will I know that my order has been placed successfully?

  • Once you successfully place your order, you will receive a confirmation email with details of your order.

  • You’ll receive another email once your order is shipped. After that, just relax and wait for your products to arrive soon!


I tried placing my order using my Debit Card/ Credit Card/ Net Banking/Wallet. The order was not successful but the amount got deducted. What do I do?

  • Kindly check your bank account to ensure if your account has been debited. If your account has been debited after a payment failure, it is normally refunded by banks within 10 business days.

  • For any other assistance/issues you can write to us at support@urbanthrift.in, and we’ll fix the issue for you.
